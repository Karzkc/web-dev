margin collapse

when two or more adjacent boxes have margin , then it becomes one margin . so box 1 and box 2 have margin 10px and box 3 and box 4 have margin 15px . box 1 and box 3 have margin 25px. it means that after applyinh margin coll.. the space between two box must be of bigger marign


In this example, instead of having a 50px margin between the two boxes (20px + 30px), the margin will collapse to 30px, which is the larger of the two margins. there is no syntax but this feature is default
